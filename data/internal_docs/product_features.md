# Product Features

## What Nimbus does
Nimbus is a team workspace for organizing projects, documents, and tasks in one
place. Core building blocks are **Workspaces**, **Projects**, **Documents**, and
**Tasks**.

## Projects
Projects group related documents, tasks, and members. Each project has
configurable access, a timeline view, and an activity feed. Projects can be
archived to hide them without deleting their contents.

## Documents
Real-time collaborative documents support rich text, tables, embeds, and
comments. Version history is retained for 90 days on Pro and indefinitely on
Business and Enterprise. Documents can be exported to PDF and Markdown.

## Tasks and boards
Tasks have assignees, due dates, labels, and statuses. They can be viewed as a
list, a Kanban board, or a calendar. Automations can move tasks between statuses
when conditions are met (Business and above).

## Search
Global search covers documents, tasks, and comments the user has access to.
Search respects permissions — results never include content a user cannot view.

## Templates
Built-in and custom templates speed up creating projects and documents. Admins
can publish workspace-wide templates.

## Plan differences (feature highlights)
- **Starter:** core projects, documents, and tasks for up to 5 members.
- **Pro:** unlimited members, integrations, 90-day version history.
- **Business:** SSO, automations, audit log, unlimited version history.
- **Enterprise:** SCIM, data residency options, priority support, custom limits.

## Mobile and offline
Native iOS and Android apps are available. Documents opened recently are cached
for read-only offline viewing; edits sync when connectivity returns.
