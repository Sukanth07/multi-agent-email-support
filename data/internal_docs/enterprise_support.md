# Enterprise Support

## Support tiers
| Plan | Channels | First-response target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | Help center, community | — |
| Pro | Email | 24 business hours |
| Business | Email, in-app chat | 8 business hours |
| Enterprise | Email, chat, priority queue, dedicated CSM | 1 hour (P1, 24/7) |

## Service level agreement (Enterprise)
Enterprise contracts include a 99.9% monthly uptime SLA. Severity levels:
- **P1 — Critical:** production down or major data risk. 1-hour response, 24/7.
- **P2 — High:** major feature impaired, no workaround. 4 business hours.
- **P3 — Normal:** minor issue or question. 1 business day.
- **P4 — Low:** feature request or guidance. 2 business days.

If an uptime SLA is missed, customers may request service credits per the
agreement.

## Dedicated Customer Success Manager
Enterprise accounts are assigned a CSM who coordinates onboarding, quarterly
business reviews, and escalations. The CSM is the named contact for roadmap and
account questions.

## Onboarding and migration
Enterprise onboarding includes a guided setup of SSO and SCIM, an optional data
migration from an existing tool, and admin training sessions.

## Requesting priority support
Enterprise admins open priority tickets from **Admin → Support → New priority
request** or by emailing the dedicated alias provided during onboarding. Include
severity, impact, affected workspace, and steps to reproduce.

## Security and legal
Enterprise customers can request the SOC 2 report, complete security
questionnaires, and execute a Data Processing Agreement (DPA). Contact the CSM or
security@nimbus.com.
