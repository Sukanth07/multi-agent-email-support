# Billing

## Plans and pricing
Nimbus is billed per active member, per month.

| Plan | Price (per member / month) | Billing cycle |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | Free (up to 5 members) | — |
| Pro | $10 | Monthly or annual |
| Business | $20 | Monthly or annual |
| Enterprise | Custom | Annual only |

Annual billing receives a 20% discount versus monthly.

## Payment methods
Pro and Business accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Enterprise
accounts may pay by ACH or wire transfer against an invoice with NET-30 terms.
Payment is processed by our PCI-DSS compliant payment provider; Nimbus does not
store full card numbers.

## Upgrading and downgrading
Plan changes take effect immediately. Upgrades are prorated for the remainder of
the current cycle. Downgrades apply at the start of the next cycle; any unused
balance is issued as account credit.

## Adding or removing members
When a member is added mid-cycle, a prorated charge applies. When a member is
removed, a prorated credit is applied to the next invoice. Billing is based on
active members only; deactivated members are not charged.

## Invoices and receipts
Invoices are emailed to the billing contact and available under
**Admin → Billing → Invoices**. Workspace Owners and Billing Admins can update the
billing email, company name, tax ID (VAT), and address used on invoices.

## Failed payments
If a charge fails, Nimbus retries on days 1, 3, and 7. After three failures the
workspace moves to a read-only state until payment succeeds. Data is retained for
60 days before any deletion.

## Refunds
Monthly plans are non-refundable. Annual plans may be refunded on a prorated basis
within 14 days of purchase. Contact billing support to request a refund.
